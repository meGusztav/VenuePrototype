Inquiry form

For venues that want to stay “not online” — this is a shareable link that captures structured inquiries.

This prototype stores data in your browser (localStorage). For a real product, you’d add authentication + database.

What happens next

  1. Inquiry lands in Admin “Inbox”.
  2. Admin proposes dates and can create a Pencil Booking (3-day hold).
  3. Payment status tracked (deposit / partial / paid).
  4. Once paid, booking becomes Confirmed and blocks the calendar.
Anonymous-friendly
You can share the inquiry link publicly without exposing your internal calendar.

How to use this prototype

  • Share the Public Inquiry page link to collect structured leads.
  • Use Admin Dashboard as your internal system (like your Sheet + messages).
  • Convert inquiries to Pencil Hold to prevent double-booking.
  • Mark Confirmed once paid.
